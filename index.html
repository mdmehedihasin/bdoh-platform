Competitions that simulate the real thing

A Year of Excellence: The 2025 Global Triumphs of Bangladeshi Olympiadians

In 2025, the BD IOI team secured a 57th global rank finish, the BD IMO team won 3 Bronze medals and 3 Honourable Mentions, and the BD IBO team took home multiple Bronze medals. Adding to the streak, the BD IPhO team earned 3 Honourable Mentions, the BD IOAA team won 1 Silver medal, 2 Honorable Mentions, alongside a Best Group Award, and finally, the BD IRO team secured an impressive 11 medals in total, including 1 Gold, 6 Bronze, and 4 technical awards.
